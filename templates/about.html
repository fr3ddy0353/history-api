Hi, I'm Frederick!

I'm a 13-year-old developer passionate about history and technology. I built History Explorer to make learning about major historical events and wars easier, more accessible, and actually fun for students and teachers.

This platform pulls live data from Wikipedia and presents it in a clean, easy-to-use format. Whether you're studying for a test or just curious about the past, History Explorer is here to help.

I plan to keep adding new content, features, and design improvements over time. If you have suggestions or ideas, I’d love to hear from you!

You can explore events, toggle dark mode, and use it on mobile or desktop — all free and open to everyone.
