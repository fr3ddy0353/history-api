About History Explorer

Hello! I’m Frederick, a 13-year-old developer passionate about history and technology. I created History Explorer to make learning about major historical wars easier and more accessible for everyone — especially students and teachers.

This project uses trusted public sources to provide clear, organized summaries and detailed sections about each conflict. My goal is to help people explore history in a way that’s simple, informative, and engaging.

If you’re a teacher, student, or school representative interested in using History Explorer, collaborating, or just want to reach out, I’d love to hear from you!
